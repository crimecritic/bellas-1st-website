    • Designer, Archivist, Artist:

    I’ve dedicated countless hours to creating, be it for a client or myself, and I have no intention of stopping. As a hard-working, competitive, and ambitious individual, I love to make myself seen through my work, all the while adapting to rapidly changing design trends. I am a particularly young designer with over five years of experience in the field, and because of this, I contribute valuable, fresh perspectives to professional design atmospheres. Through my work in the field as well as within design education, I’ve honed my craft immensely and pride myself on being able to create for a living.

    I specialize in utilizing archival, free-use materials and hand-drawn artwork in my design. As a lover of history and classical design styles, I find myself frequently looking into the past to create the artwork of the future. While I can utilize artificial intelligence in my design work, I will always prioritize real, handcrafted design at every available moment.
